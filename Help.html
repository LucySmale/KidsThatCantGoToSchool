Learn and share knowledge

Start by educating yourself, then talk about it with your friends, teachers, or family. Post facts online or create a presentation at school. Every time you share, more people become aware of the problem.

Support charities

Organizations like UNICEF, Save the Children, or local charities work to get kids into school. You can help by donating, even small amounts, or by joining school fundraisers. Remember, even the cost of a movie ticket could buy supplies for a child.

Take action in your school

You and your friends could run an awareness campaign. Make posters, organize a fundraiser, or hold a special assembly. You could even create a short video to show your classmates.

Use your voice

Write to leaders, create petitions, or make posts online. Sometimes raising awareness is just as powerful as giving money. Young people have strong voices that deserve to be heard.

Be creative

Use art, writing, music, or social media to share this issue. A powerful drawing, TikTok, or short story can reach more people than you think.
